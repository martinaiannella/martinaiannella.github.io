Martina Iannella

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute of Discrete Mathematics and Geometry of the TU Wien.

I received my PhD in October 2023 at the University of Udine under the supervision of Alberto Marcone and Vincenzo Dimonte.

My research areas are Mathematical Logic and Set Theory. My research focuses on descriptive set theory, large cardinals and generalized descriptive set theory.
